Radius is the distance from the center of the circle out to its edge. Every other measurement below is derived from it.

Formulas

  • C = 2πr
  • d = 2r
  • C = πd
  • A = πr²

r = radius (center to edge).
d = diameter through the center.
C = circumference around the circle.
A = area inside the circle.
π ≈ 3.14159, the ratio of circumference to diameter.
r² = r × r.

What π means

π ≈ 3.14159265
